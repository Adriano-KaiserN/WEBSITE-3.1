Admission Requirements

Admission Process

To apply for admission to Multimedia University, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit our official website and navigate to the admissions portal
  2. Create an account with your personal details
  3. Fill in the online application form
  4. Upload the required documents
  5. Pay the application fee
  6. Submit your application

Required Documents

  • High School Certificate
  • National ID/Passport
  • Passport Photos
  • Application Fee Receipt

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: December 31, 2025
  • Orientation: January 15, 2026
  • Classes Begin: January 20, 2026

Entry Requirements

Minimum entry requirements for undergraduate programs:

  • KCSE Mean Grade of C+ and above
  • Minimum grade of C+ in relevant subjects
  • Diploma holders from recognized institutions
  • Equivalent qualifications from international examination bodies

For more information about admissions, please visit our official website or contact the admissions office.
